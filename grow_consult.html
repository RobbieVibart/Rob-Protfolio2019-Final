Grow Consulting -Absa

Project description

I was approched by Grow consultancy
to take an already conceptulised ABSA
(bank) board game. That would be used as
internal training tool that would
intoroduce newly recruited staff -both
managers and branch owners. The board
would educate and inrich the
owners of how to manage and maintain
thier individual branches/Retail-outlets
and of the inner workings and day to
day challenges that they would face.
