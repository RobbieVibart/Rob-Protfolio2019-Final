Grow Consulting- RBB

Project description

Print | Design | Branding | Research
I was approched by Grow consultancy to take an already conceptulised ABSA (bank) board game. That would be used as an internal training tool that would intoroduce newly recruited staff -both managers and branch owners.

The board would educate and inrich the owners of how to manage and maintain thier individual branches/Retail-outlets and of the inner workings and day to day challenges that they would face@.

colour palette

The board games colour palette was strictly ABSA CI documentation which was provided by Grow Consulting. All items that were requested included, the iconography the game cards, the answer and knowledge sheet and the board game itself all of these items used thier CI and colour palette as illustrated above.

Icon design & construction

The Iconography that was created required a construction guideline, as this was a request with in the brief that was supplied. The guidline as illustrated below would show how to maintain a consistent look and feel through-out the various designed icons. That were used both on the board game and on the various other items.

This would emphasisize the look and feel consistently.

game cards

The game cards were split into four sets. Each represented different challenges and or tasks that were required to be completed by the players.

The iconography that was illustrated on the face and used within the construction guidelines. The cards, that would visually display what the category of the task /challenge, If the players had to land on a task icon that was scatted through-out the game. This was a requirement set durning the initial brief and concept phase.
