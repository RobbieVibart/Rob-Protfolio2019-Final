Once on Malta

Once On Malta(1SON Malta), is a company based in Malta the company required a website that had simplicity in mind and would highlight the companies services and various travel requirments. the main functionality of the website would serve an a section where the client/s could view certain events held in Malta and apply for travel during the eventing date and communicate via an simple contact form. The website was created using Wordpress.
