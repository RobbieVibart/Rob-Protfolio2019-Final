Lending Lola

Overview

First National Bank is a renowned and reputable banking institution based in South Africa.

The marketing team responsible for their lending and Private Bankingconsultancy or FNB PBL (Private Bank Lending) division needed an informative and internal communication strategy. This would educate other marketing sectors within the bank of an informative way of maximising communication and workflow delivery with the banks Private clients. A female avatar or mascot was conceptualised but the team needed a visual perspective of this. The Avatar concept was dubbed "Lending Lola".

Solution

The PBL team supplied me with an illustrated image of Lola. I had designed Informational leaflets of how the Lending Lola process would function A static mailer as an email invitation, a mock-up of and a Tinder post that would later be used as a pre-invite on Valentines Day prior to the set event date.

This would maximise communication and offered a creative targeting strategy. The use of their corporate identity was used, but was not strictly applied as this was purely targeted to the banks internal staff.

An additional type face was included to add a
playful visual element and likeness of the Lola
mascot.

Conclusion

The rollout of this new concept and unveiling of the Lending Lola launch was event driven, with the use of print media and the "Tinder like post". Drove the required market. And later was implemented as a internal staff training programme
