ODA
visual Identity

overview


ODA
(Organisational Development Africa) is a privately owned strategy and change management consultancy specialising in complex, large-scale institutional and social change processes. ODA requested that I firstly conceptulize a logo and then required a full Identity guidline that would be applied to all areas of their internal stationery and if required any future branding -be that promotional leaflets to signage requirements. A logo mood board was applied so that the client could verify that the correct visual treatment was given.

solution

Once the client was happy with the direction of the logo. The identity guidelines were conceptualised and applied to a grid system that was created and used so that the guideline, once constructed kept true to the brands look and feel. This would maintain consistency through-out on all branding.
