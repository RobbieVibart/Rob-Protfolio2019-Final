Print | Design | Branding | Research
I was approched by Grow Consultancy to take an already conceptulised board game. That would be used as an internal training tool that would intoroduce newly recruited staff -both managers and branch owners. The board would educate and inrich the owners of how to manage and maintain thier individual branches/Retail-outlets and of the workings and day to day challenges that they would face.

The board games colour palette was strictly ABSA CI documentation which was provided by Grow Consulting. All items that were requested included, the iconography the game cards, the answer and knowledge sheet and the board game itself all of these items used their CI and colour palette as illustrated above. The Iconography that was created required a construction guideline, as this was a request with in the brief that was supplied. The guideline as illustrated below would show how to maintain a consistent look and feel through-out the various designed icons. That were used both on the board game and on the various other items. This would emphasise the look and feel consistently.
