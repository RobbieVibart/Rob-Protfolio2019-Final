Logofolio

All the following are both logo and logo marks completed for various projects, both corporate and private designs, that I had completed over the years.

Reclaim and Remain is an events company. The client requested a logo that would combine research and eventing and a crisp type face that would reflect the companies bold perspective.

Unako requested a logo for the Kenyan National Lottery. The client requested a logo that would encompass the Kenyan people in an abstract form, and their unique culture.

To construct the logo I was supplied various Kenyan artworks. I had chosen to construct the logo from a Kenyan Maasia mask, and deconstruct the abstract forms to assemble the final logo.

Just Wrapped is a supplier of homeware packaged corporate gifts The client requested a logo that would encompass an elegant, modern and continuos feel with the use of a ribbon like design.

Lottomestari is a privately owned company based in Malta. I was requested to design and conceptualise a logo or mark The would visually communicate to an online gaming platform. The company also required a mascot or character that would communicate its position within the market.

The Golden ratio design principle was followed to construct the mascot and digitally recreated to finalize the design of mascot.

Ledimar is a South African investments company The client requested a logo that postioned its self in an already saturated market. The client was very specific that the image of the company had to show wealth and bravery with in a highly competative market.
